The series revolves around four older, single women (three widos adn one divorcée) sharing a house in Miami, Florida. The owner of the house is a widow named Blanche Deveraux (Rue McClanahan), who was joined by fellow wido Rose Nylund (Betty White) and divorcée Dorothy Zbornak (Bea Arthur). THey both responded to a room-for-rent ad on the bullentin board of a local grocery store. In the pilot episode, the women had a gay cook named Coco (Charles Levin), who was subsequently removed.[5] The three were soon joined by Dorothy's mother, Sophia Petrillo (Estelle Getty), after the retirement home where she lived, Shady Pines, burned down.[6][7]
